    Getting myUdutu course

    To import your myUdutu course, you must login with your myUdutu username and password after clicking the "Get myUdutu Course" button. Once you've done this, you can choose the course that you want to import. Your course will be prepared, and you will be notified when it is ready.

    If you don't have a myUdutu username and password, you can create a free account at www.myudutu.com
